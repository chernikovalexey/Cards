Welcome to the tutorial
Your goal: place blocks in the space so, that after applying physics to them, they fall in the way energy flows through them from the blue cube to the orange one.
Use your mouse wheel or arrows to rotate the block
Hold Alt and left-click to zoom camera in
Hold Alt+Z and left-click to zoom camera out
Hold Space and drag the mouse to move the camera. Or, use WASD instead.
Press Ctrl+Shift to apply physics or rewind (in case physics are already applied).
Press Ctrl+Z to undo the most recent action.
